# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of expertise.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, evidence, and reproducibility.
- Acknowledge contributions and sources.
- Protect confidential, clinical, and personally identifying information.
- Clearly distinguish deployable methods from oracle or diagnostic analyses.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, deliberate disclosure of private information, falsification of results, and uploading restricted clinical data are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's security-advisory channel when confidentiality is required. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when this policy is violated.
